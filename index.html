Lynyrd

Aka:Len Dawg

Len is a five year old black lab beagle mix. He is a bit shy an anxious but once he gets to know you he'll quickly become your best friend. He is a very happy and playful boy who loves going to the river for a swim or down to the park to play some fetch. He use to be a free-range dog but as of late he is not to be trusted around the garbage un-supervised.

Len loves the snow!!

Winter is that good stick weather!! The frozen sticks are easier to chomp into pieces, and Len loves him some chompin. I let him carry a stick home from the park almost evey day. He carries them home and adds them to his vast stick collection..all the neighborhood dogs are so jealous.

Len loves all things dirty!!

When we go on walks after it rains I have to really keep an eye on him, or he'll lay down in a dirty mud puddle. He also tries to dig holes in the baseball field if your not watching, and I can't figure out if he does it because he likes diggin, or he's tired of those kids ruining his fetch time.
