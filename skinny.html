Skynyrd

Aka:skinny

Skynyrd is a 8 year old indoor/outdoor cat. She is a very sucessfull hunter and leave's decapitated or dismembered critters on my porch almost every day. Although she is a cold blooded killer when it comes to small rodents, shes loves people and most other animals (except sweetie pie).

Skinny loves boxes!!

No matter how small the box she will try and fit. I've bought her numerous cat beds over the years and she refuses to use them. But if you leave an empty amazon box on the floor she is in heaven. I leave an empty shoe box on top of my kitchen island and she sleeps in it almost every night.

Skinny loves being outside

I live in a very small town so I let her go outside and she loves it!! She spends most of her day out there and loves to follow me and lynyrd when we walk down to the park. All my neighbors know her and she loves haning out on their porches with them because she knows they'll give her treats!!
